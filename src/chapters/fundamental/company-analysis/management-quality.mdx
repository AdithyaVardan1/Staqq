# Management Quality

The best business with poor management often underperforms an average business with great management. People matter.

<InfoBox type="note">
  As Peter Lynch said: "Go for a business that any idiot can run – because sooner or later, any idiot probably will be running it." But great management can multiply returns.
</InfoBox>

## Why Management Matters

Management:
- Allocates capital
- Makes strategic decisions
- Sets culture and values
- Navigates challenges
- Executes on vision

</InfoBox>

## Management Changes

When leadership changes:
- Understand why
- Assess new leader's track record
- Watch for strategy shifts
- Give time before judging

## Key Takeaways

- Management quality significantly impacts returns
- Track record of capital allocation is crucial
- Alignment through ownership and sensible pay
- Governance quality protects minority shareholders
- Watch for red flags in related parties and audit
