# Pattern Reliability

Not all patterns work. In fact, many fail. Understanding pattern reliability helps you filter good trades from bad ones.

## Why Patterns Fail
1. **Market Context:** A bullish pattern in a strong bear market has lower odds.
2. **Low Volume:** Breakouts on low volume are often "fakeouts".
3. **News Events:** Unexpected news overrides technicals.

## The Filter System

Before taking a trade based on a pattern, apply these filters:

### 1. The Trend Filter
"With the trend" patterns work 60-70% of the time. "Counter-trend" patterns work 30-40% of the time.
- **Rule:** Only take Bullish patterns if price is above the 200 EMA.

### 2. The Volume Filter
Volume must confirm the move.
- **Breakout:** Needs HIGH volume.
- **Pullback:** Needs LOW volume.

### 3. The Location Filter
Where is the pattern forming?
- **Golden Zone:** Pattern forming AT a major support/resistance level.
- **No Man's Land:** Pattern forming in the middle of the range (Avoid).
