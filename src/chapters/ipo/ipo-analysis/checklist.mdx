## Final Questions

Before you apply:

1. **Would I buy this at 20% above issue price?**
   - If no, listing gains are your only hope
   
2. **Would I hold this if it lists at discount?**
   - If no, reconsider your conviction
   
3. **Have I relied on my own research?**
   - If just following tips, step back

<InfoBox type="warning">
  Fear of missing out (FOMO) drives poor IPO decisions. If you're rushing because "everyone is applying," that's a warning sign.
</InfoBox>

## Key Takeaways

- Use this checklist for every IPO, without exception
- Score each area systematically
- Red flags should trigger deep investigation
- Be honest about your reasons for investing
- When in doubt, it's okay to skip
