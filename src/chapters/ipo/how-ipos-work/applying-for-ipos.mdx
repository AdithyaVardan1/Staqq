# Applying for IPOs

Applying for an IPO has evolved from filling physical forms to a 2-click process. However, the strategy for getting an allotment remains a game of probability.

## Investor Categories
You must choose a "bucket" to apply in. Each has a reserved quota.

### 1. Retail Individual Investor (RII)
- **Who:** General public.
- **Limit:** Investment up to ₹2 Lakhs.
- **Quota:** 35% of the total issue (usually).
- **Allotment Logic:** Lottery System.
  - If oversubscribed, 1 lot is given to lucky winners. Applying for ₹2 Lakhs (max bid) **does not** increase your chance of winning.
  - **Strategy:** Apply for **1 Lot only**. Use multiple Demat accounts of family members (PAN cards) to increase probability.

### 2. High Net-worth Individual (HNI / NII)
- **Who:** Individuals investing > ₹2 Lakhs.
- **Quota:** 15%.
- **Small HNI (sHNI):** ₹2L to ₹10L.
- **Big HNI (bHNI):** > ₹10L.
- **Allotment Logic:** Pro-rata (Proportional).
  - If subscribed 100x, you need to apply for 100 lots to get 1 lot guaranteed.
  - **Strategy:** Requires massive capital funding (IPO Financing). Not recommended for beginners.

### 3. Qualified Institutional Buyer (QIB)
- **Who:** Mutual Funds, Banks, FIIs.
- **Quota:** 50%.
- **Significance:** If QIBs are not buying, stay away. They are the "Smart Money".

## The Application Process (UPI + ASBA)
1. **Login** to Zerodha/Upstox/Groww.
2. Select IPO -> **Apply**.
3. **Important:** Always check the **"Cut-off Price"** checkbox.
   - IPOs have a price band (e.g., ₹100 - ₹108).
   - If you bid at ₹100 and the final price is ₹108, your application is rejected instantly.
   - Cut-off ensures you bid at the final decided price.
4. Enter **UPI ID** and submit.
5. **CRITICAL:** Open your UPI App (Gpay/PhonePe) and **Approve the Mandate**. Without this, your application is incomplete.

## Timelines
- **Day 1-3:** Bidding Window. (Tip: Apply on Day 2 or 3 after checking QIB subscription data).
- **Day 6:** Finalization of Basis of Allotment (Check status on registrar websites like Link Intime/Kfintech).
- **Day 8:** Listing on NSE/BSE.
