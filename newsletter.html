Concept of the Week
GMP — Grey Market Premium
What it is · Why it lies sometimes
GMP is the price at which an IPO's shares trade before the stock officially lists on an exchange — in an unofficial, unregulated grey market. A GMP of +₹20 on a ₹406 IPO suggests the market expects it to list around ₹426.

The catch: GMP is driven by speculation and sentiment, not fundamentals. It can swing wildly in the final days before listing, and has been wrong plenty of times. Use it as a signal, not a guarantee. Staqq updates GMP every 5 minutes on our IPO Hub.
