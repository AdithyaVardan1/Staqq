💡 Concept of the week
GMP — Grey Market Premium
What it is · Why it lies sometimes
GMP is the price at which an IPO's shares trade before the stock officially lists. It is driven by sentiment and speculation. A +₹20 GMP suggests positive listing expectations.

The catch: It's unregulated and can swing wildly. Use it as a signal, not a guarantee.
